Lyric Check — borrowed / regurgitated line finder

the other half of "did they write it": this catches stolen lines (real songs ReMi regurgitated or someone copied) that read as human and slip past AI-detection.
● model: off
Paste a lyric and hit Check lines.
What this is. It can't hold every song ever written, so it flags lines the local model already recognizes — strong recognition means the line is either a memorized real lyric (borrowed) or generic filler. Use the per-line search buttons to confirm a hit against published lyrics. It pairs with the AI-detector: that tool catches generated lyrics; this one catches stolen ones. Neither is proof — they're leads. Needs the local model for scoring (works as a search-helper without it).
